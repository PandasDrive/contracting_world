High Risk

6-18 months typical duration

Cost Plus Contracts (CPFF/CPIF)

Cost Plus Fixed Fee / Cost Plus Incentive Fee

Risk Level

Low Risk

Government pays actual costs plus fee

Timeline

1-5 years typical duration

Best For

R&D, uncertain requirements, complex projects

Watch Out For

Cost control, documentation requirements, audit scrutiny

Time & Materials (T&M)

Pay for time worked and materials used

Risk Level

Medium Risk

Shared risk between contractor and government

‚è± Timeline

3-12 months typical duration

Best For

Short-term projects, maintenance, emergency work

Watch Out For

Hourly rate caps, material markup limits, time tracking

Indefinite Delivery Contracts (IDIQ)

3-10 years with option periods

Watch Out For
