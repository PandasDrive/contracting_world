Research Market Rates
Use ClearanceJobs salary data and Glassdoor to understand current market rates for your role and location.
Highlight Your Value
Emphasize your clearance level, specialized skills, and relevant experience that directly benefits the contract.
Consider Total Package
Look beyond base salary - consider benefits, PTO, remote work options, and professional development opportunities.

Negotiate when you have leverage - during contract re-competes or when you have multiple offers.
Be Professional
Maintain a collaborative tone. Remember, you're building a long-term relationship with the company.
